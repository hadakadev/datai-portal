Infrastructure

The Technology
behind DatAI network

Datai is powered by a modular architecture with three layers that integrate to provide decentralized, intelligent data infrastructure, leveraging Datais ready-to-use intelligence for actionable on-chain AI insights.

Application Layer

End-User Applications

This layer contains all customer-facing and internal applications. It includes compliance and AML tools, financial data apps, and user behavior analysis.

DATA REFINING LAYER

ML & AI Integration

After data is fetched by the indexer layer, it is processed into the Datai LLM. Here, Datai ML Analytics and AI models are applied to clean and classify smart contracts, converting the data from a raw structured format into insightful information.

DATA SOURCING LAYER

Core Functionality

This is the backbone of Datai Network where structured data is indexed. It consists of a decentralized indexer layer where node operators run indexer nodes, and developers add protocol & DApp integrations, allowing for infinite scalability of data coverage.

F.A.Q.Learn more
about DatAI

1
What is Datai Network?
Datai Network is a decentralized data and analytics platform that aggregates blockchain data, providing powerful insights and analytics across multiple verticals, including DeFi, NFTs, Real-World Assets, and GameFi.
2
How does Datai Network work?
The Datai Network is a group of interconnected indexers that work together to collect and share blockchain data. These indexers, called nodes, gather information from various blockchains and help users access this data when they need it. If a node doesn't have all the requested information, it connects with other nodes to fulfill the request. By participating in the network and helping to provide data, nodes earn rewards. This system ensures that blockchain information is readily available and up-to-date for anyone who needs it.
3
How is data obtained from Datai Network more insightful than simply fetching it from an RPC node?
Datai Network leverages machine learning to automatically label and categorize smart contracts, ensuring real-time data processing and analytics. Our decentralized infrastructure allows for scalability and reliability in data access.
4
What are the benefits of using Datai Network?
By utilizing Datai Network, users can access comprehensive blockchain data quickly, gain actionable insights, and benefit from our extensive coverage across various blockchain protocols and sectors.
5
How can I use the Datai token ($DATAI)?
The $DATAI token can be used for staking, accessing premium data services, and participating in governance decisions within the Datai Network ecosystem.
6
Who are the customers of Datai Network?
Our platform is trusted by leading companies in the blockchain space, including Ledger, Zerion, Etherspot, and many others, who rely on our accurate and reliable data.
7
How can I get involved with Datai Network?
You can join our community by participating in discussions on our social media channels, contributing to our ecosystem, or becoming a token holder.
8
What is the vision behind Datai Network?
Our vision is to empower individuals and organizations with seamless access to decentralized data, transforming how blockchain insights are generated and utilized across industries.
9
How did Datai Network start out?
Datai Network originally started out as Merlin, a centralized blockchain data provider, delivering precise DeFi insights to key market players. As the demand for blockchain data grew and the scope expanded beyond DeFi and NFTs, the team quickly realized that they needed a more scalable, decentralized approach to cover additional verticals like Real-World Assets (RWA), GameFi, and more. By transitioning to a decentralized approach, the aim is to scale data coverage, improve reliability, and grow the blockchain data ecosystem faster.

How can I stay updated on Datai Network's developments?
You can subscribe to our newsletter, follow us on social media, or check our roadmap for the latest news and updates.
